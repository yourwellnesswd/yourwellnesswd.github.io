Healthy Foods

Having healthy food will improve your over all wellness. Healthy foods do not have to be all gross and disgusting. Healthy foods consist of vitamans and minerals that will help balance everything out. Having a healthy relationship with food is just as important as anything else. But it is also just as important to eat foods that will benefit you. Just snacking on snacks all the time won't cut it. As most know there are certain categories of food that people should be digesting. The 5 categories below will give your body the nutrients it needs, and they should all be eaten throughout the day. The foods/drinks that were listed are not the only foods in the categories, the lists goes on forever, but those were just some ideas.

Dairy

Milk, Yogurt, Cheese, Chocolate Milk, Smoothies, and Pudding.


Vegetables

Broccoli, Asparagus, Bell Peppers, Corn, Avacodo, Carrots, Sweet Potatoes, and Tomatoes.


Fruits

Strawberries, Apples, Bananas, Blueberries, Orange juice, Watermelon, Grapes, and Mango.


Grains

Tortillas, Cereal, Rice, Bread, Pasta, Popcorn, Crackers, Bagels, and Oatmeal.


Protein

Eggs, Tuna (fish), Hamburgers/Cheeseburgers, Nuts, Chicken, Tofu, Beans, Salmon, and Porkchops.
