»Diets

Diets can be healthy, and give your body what it needs to get you through your day.
The most important things your body needs is a source of: carbs, protein, fat, fiber, vitamins,
minerals and water. All though there are MANY different kinds of diets, you get to choose which one
would help you the most. Diets aren't always about loosing weight, they help you get the right amount of
foods you need. Take a look at some, and choose what YOU feel your body needs, or just to try something new.

»Motivation

Motivation. Like getting a reward after you win something. Keeping up with your body physically can be much easier
if you have something to work for. Example, you can treat yourself to a spa, or a free day, or even a sweet snack.
If you have something to look forwards to, you probably will want to do whatever you can to do it.
You also can have motivation that isn't tangible. You can have a mindset that keeping your body physically healthy
will have a postive effect on your life. Motivation is all about having energy to keep up with yourself.
