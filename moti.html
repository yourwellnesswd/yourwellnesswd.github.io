Motivations


Motivations are used to give you a boost. Getting into a habit of something
can be very difficult, especially if you feel it takes a lot to get something done. Having motivations will make that process much easier. Motivations can be anything from a nice reward at the end of the week (a sweet snack), or even a bonus trip to somewhere nice...maybe a Italy trip at the end of long work year. Motivations don't have to be rewards or something you get after completing a goal, they can be daily things, small things. Take music as an example, it gets you going, and gets you in the mood to get things done. That is what motivations are supposed to be.
