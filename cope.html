Coping Mechanisms


There is no perfect way to cope with anything, but knowing effective ways will give your mental health an upgrade. Some people deal with difficult times or difficult situations by doing very harmful things. There are many different ways to cope, for example, talking to someone, talking to a therapist, writing your feelings down, these are all healthy ways to cope. Some struggle, and so they breakdown and get left in a dark spot. If you feel like you are trapped with yourself, it is usually a sign that you need to let go, and take some weight off your shoulders. If you are the type of person that likes to keep things private, you have to relase these feelings in some way, letting them sit there and build up will only drag you down. Having a journal that is only yours is a perfect fine way to let go of things while still keeping them private. There ARE healthy ways to cope.
